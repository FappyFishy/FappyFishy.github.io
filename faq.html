Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. Go to: Print HZ
  2. Type in your HZ username and password and click on "Login".
  3. Click on "Choose File" and select the file you want to print.
  4. Click on "Next".
  5. Choose the HZ printer or HZ plotter to print your document(s).
  6. Use your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the printer.
  7. Next, select the printer option "Print / Afdrukken".
  8. Next, you can see which file is ready to be printed.
  9. The print job will only be completed, if you have sufficient credit on your printing account.
  10. When the printing is completed, press "Logout".

Q:How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  1. Register using your HZ card on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer
  2. Lay the original sheet on the glass plate.
  3. Press "Scan and Send".
  4. Press "Yes".
  5. Press the "Start" button
  6. Press the "Start" button.
  7. When the scanning is complete press "Logout".

Q:How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

  1. Visit the HZ Web Shop
  2. Create an Account: If you don't already have an account on the HZ web shop, you may need to create one.
  3. Browse Products or Events: You may find it under "Events," "Courses," or a similar category.
  4. Select the IT Introduction Event
  5. Add to Cart
  6. View Cart
  7. Proceed to Checkout
  8. Payment: Enter your payment details.

Q: How can you book a project room in one of the wings?

Head to the selfservice section of Portal HZ click "Book a project room" and follow the given instructions.
