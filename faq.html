Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. First you need to go to the website: Print HZ
  2. Then you type in your HZ username and password and after that you need to click on "Login".
  3. Click on "Choose File" and select the file you want to print.
  4. Click on "Next". Repeat the previous step if you want to add more documents.
  5. Wait until the document has been processed and then click the Advanced option to state whether you want double- single sided or black & white printing.
  6. After, click on the HZ printer or HZ plotter to print your document(s).
  7. Once the print job has been requested you must register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer.
  8. Next, select the printer option "Print / Afdrukken".
  9. When you're in the menu, select the multifunctional printer to where the job has been sent.
  10. Next, you can see which file is ready to be printed.
  11. The print job will only be completed, if you have sufficient credit on your printing account.
  12. When the printing is completed, press "Stop" on the TouchID, followed by "Logout".

Q:How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  1. Ensure that you have atleast €0.07 credits. (Although scanning is free, you must have that amount on your HZ card).
  2. Register using your HZ card on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer
  3. Lay the original sheet on the feeder of the glass plate.
  4. Press "Scan and Send".
  5. Press "Scan to me".
  6. Press "Yes".
  7. Press the "Start" button
  8. When you see the screen for scanning via the glass plate, press the "Start" button.
  9. Once this is ready, press "Start Sending"
  10. When the scanning is complete, press "Stop" on the TouchID, followed by "Logout".

Q:How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

  1. Visit the HZ Web Shop
  2. Create an Account: If you don't already have an account on the HZ web shop, you may need to create one. This often involves providing your personal information and creating a username and password.
  3. Browse Products or Events: Navigate to the section or category that includes the IT introduction event or the item you want to purchase. You may find it under "Events," "Courses," or a similar category.
  4. Select the IT Introduction Event: Click on the specific IT introduction event or item you want to purchase. This should take you to a product page with more details.
  5. Add to Cart: On the product page, there should be an option to "Add to Cart". Click this button to add the event or item to your shopping cart.
  6. View Cart: After adding the event or item to your cart, you can usually view your cart to review the items you've added. Make sure everything is correct, and you can adjust quantities if necessary.
  7. Proceed to Checkout: Once you're ready to make your purchase, proceed to the checkout page. This is where you'll provide your payment and billing information.
  8. Payment: Enter your payment details. You may be able to pay using various methods such as credit/debit cards or other online payment options. Follow the instructions provided on the checkout page to complete the payment process.
  9. Place Your Order: If everything looks correct, you can place your order.
  10. Confirmation: After successfully placing your order, you should receive an order confirmation. This may be displayed on the website, sent to your email address, or both. Keep this confirmation for your records.
  11. Attend the Event: If you've purchased a ticket or registration for an event like the IT introduction, make sure to mark the event date on your calendar and follow any additional instructions provided.
